Thank you for your interest in sponsoring this blog.

## What you get

A text-only sponsor banner displayed on every blog post. The banner format is:

> Sponsored by **[Your Company]** &mdash; Your message here (140 characters max)

The banner links to a URL of your choice. No JavaScript, no cookies, no tracking pixels. Just a clean text link that respects reader privacy.

## Rules

- **Text only**: no images, logos, or rich media in the banner
- **140-character limit** on the sponsor message
- **No tracking**: no JavaScript, cookies, or analytics pixels
- **No affiliate links**: direct links to your product or service only
- **Editorial independence**: sponsorship does not influence blog content
